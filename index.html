05 Case Study Reporting Time Cut by 50%
+
Demonstrates: Narrative Structure · Results-Led Writing · Business Outcomes

A performance marketing team handling multiple B2B clients was spending nearly two days every week building reports. Data came from different platforms, numbers had to be matched manually, and by the time reports were ready, the insights were already outdated.

Problem

Manual cross-platform reporting consuming two days weekly. Insights arrived too late to act on.

What Changed

Centralized reporting system connected all channels automatically. No manual exports.

Result

Reporting time dropped from two days to a few hours. Decisions made mid-campaign.

Within One Quarter
  • Reporting time reduced by over 50%
  • Underperforming campaigns adjusted mid-cycle
  • Budget allocation improved based on real-time signals
